Eight ways ICA grows a child

Each program turns careful observation into something a child actually builds, from a published book to a confident presentation. Labs run in small groups of up to four. Premium tracks are fully 1:1.

Small-group Labs · up to 4 students
Max 4 students · 60 min · 9 weeks📖

Young Independent Readers Lab

Enjoying the discovery of new words. Reaching for a book without being asked. Seeking out the books they love. Through these experiences, they build vocabulary, comprehension, the focus to read to the end, and a free-flowing imagination. Max group of 4.

Max 4 students · 90 min · 9 weeks📖

Advanced Reading & Critical Thinking Lab

Learning to read analytically and think actively. Examining structure and subtext beyond simple interpretation. Discerning the author's true intent with clear evidence. Through these experiences, they develop profound comprehension, sharp analytical skills, and rigorous critical thinking. Max group of 4.

Max 4 students · 90 min · 9 weeks🔎

Research & Presentation Lab

Formulating sharp questions and navigating diverse sources. Learning the core mechanics of structured research. Delivering personal insights with absolute confidence. Through these experiences, they master critical research skills and powerful presentation abilities to tackle any assignment. Max group of 4.

Max 4 students · 60 min · 9 weeks

Young Authors Publishing Lab with Amazon

Bringing imagined stories to life as real, published books. Designing characters and illustrations alongside a specially invited professional art educator. Publishing a finished book on Amazon. Through these experiences, they complete their own illustrated story and gain the rare pride of becoming an author. Max group of 4.

Max 4 students · 90 min · 12 weeks

Advanced Authors Publishing Lab with Amazon

Writing freely across diverse genres from fiction to poetry. Elevating book covers and illustrations to professional standards under the guidance of a specially invited professional art educator. Publishing an official book on Amazon under their own name. Through these experiences, they build a powerful creative credential and the lifelong confidence of a credited author. Max group of 4.

How it works

A simple start to a deeply personal journey

Our 1:1 tracks run as a seasonal membership: a fixed time slot reserved entirely for your child, with a lesson designed fresh each time.

1

Inquire

Reach out by email. Tell us a little about your child and what you're hoping for.

2

Diagnose

We observe how your child thinks, learns, and responds, reading the patterns beneath the scores.

3

Personalize

A curriculum is built only for your child and redrawn at every lesson, keeping their growth at the center.

4

Partner

We transparently share the whole journey with your family, so home and academy look in the exact same direction.
