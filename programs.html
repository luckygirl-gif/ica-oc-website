Eight ways ICA grows a child

Each program turns careful observation into something a child actually builds, from a published book to a confident presentation. Labs run in small groups of up to four. Premium tracks are fully 1:1.

Small-group Labs · up to 4 students
Group of 4 · 60 min · 9 weeks📖

Young Independent Readers Lab

At ICA at OC, we help each child enjoy discovering new words and reach for a book without being asked. Once reading becomes a joy, a child reads on their own and begins to seek out the books they love, building vocabulary and comprehension, the focus to read to the end, and the imagination to explore freely along the way. In a small group of no more than four, guided to each child's pace.

Group of 4 · 60 min · 9 weeks✍️

Young Authors Publishing Lab

Every child has a story they want to tell. In the Young Authors Publishing Lab, children experience their imagined stories becoming real, published books. As they write, children work alongside a specially invited professional art educator to imagine characters and create illustrations and a cover, and through that process their own story is completed as an illustrated book. The finished book is published on Amazon, and holding a book they wrote and illustrated themselves gives a child a rare pride, the pride of being an author. Offered in a small group of no more than four.

Group of 4 · 90 min · 9 weeks🧠

Advanced Reading & Critical Thinking Lab

Many children read a book yet cannot fully discern what the author truly intended. They have never learned to read analytically, or to think actively about what they read. At ICA at OC, these are treated as one discipline. A child moves from accurate interpretation to examining structure and subtext, and finally to discerning the author's intent with evidence to support it. In a small group of no more than four, taught to the level of each child's thinking.

Group of 4 · 90 min · 9 weeks🔎

Research & Presentation Mini Lab

Many children freeze when a research or presentation assignment lands. Most have never been taught how to research, or how to deliver their ideas through a presentation. At ICA at OC, we teach both: the skill of asking a question and working through sources, and the skill of presenting one's own thinking with confidence. All in a small group of no more than four, guided to each child's pace.

Group of 4 · 90 min · 12 weeks📦

Advanced Authors Publishing Lab with Amazon

In the Advanced Authors Publishing Lab, a student becomes a credited author under their own name. Writing freely across any genre they choose, fiction, mystery, prose, or poetry, each student shapes their story into a finished book. As the writing matures, a specially invited professional art educator helps bring the cover and illustrations to a finished standard. The completed book is then published on Amazon, becoming more than an experience: a creative credential the student built themselves. Offered in a small group of no more than four.
