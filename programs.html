Six ways ICA grows a child

Each program turns careful observation into something a child actually builds, from a published book to a confident presentation. Labs run in small groups of up to four. Premium tracks are fully 1:1.

Group of 4 · 9 weeks🔎

Research & Presentation Mini Lab

From a research question to sources, an evidence chart, and a mini presentation, children learn to organize their thinking and confidently articulate it.

Group of 4 · 9 weeks✍️

Story Writing & Publishing Lab

Children write and illustrate an original story, then publish it as a real book on Amazon KDP.

Group of 4 · Year-round📚

Analytical Book Reading Lab

We go beyond simple summary, reading into a text's structure, the meaning between the lines, and the author's hidden intent.

1:1 Premium🧭

Temperament & Character Coaching Lab

1:1 coaching grounded in temperament and character analysis. We understand each child's nature first, then guide their growth from there.

1:1 Premium🏡

Homeschooling Design Lab

A one-of-a-kind homeschool plan built around your family's goals and your child's own rhythm.

1:1 Premium📋

IEP Review / Parent Consulting

A careful review of your child's IEP, with consulting that helps home and ICA set the right direction together.
